Top Activities in Mallorca

Experience the best of the island — from coastal adventures to cultural gems.

Things to Do

🚤 Boat Tours

Explore Mallorca’s stunning coastline and secret coves by sea.

🥾 Mountain Hiking

Hike through the scenic Serra de Tramuntana for breathtaking views.

🏛️ Visit Palma Cathedral

Marvel at the Gothic masterpiece of La Seu in Palma’s historic center.

🍷 Wine Tasting Tours

Tour family-run vineyards and enjoy authentic Mallorcan wines.

🏖️ Beach Relaxation

Unwind at world-class beaches like Es Trenc and Cala Millor.
